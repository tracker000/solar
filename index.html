What's the Kp index? (tap)

The Kp index measures geomagnetic activity on a scale from 0-9, indicating the likelihood of seeing auroras:

  • Kp 6: Minor storm - Aurora visible at higher latitudes (Canada, Northern Europe)
  • Kp 7: Moderate storm - Aurora may be visible in northern US states, Germany, UK
  • Kp 8: Strong storm - Aurora potentially visible much further south
  • Kp 9: Extreme storm - Rare event, aurora visible at very low latitudes
